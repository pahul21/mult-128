About Elara Café

A calm and refined café brand built around simplicity, quality, and comfort.

Our Story

Elara Café was created as a modern café concept that combines elegant branding with a warm customer experience. The idea behind the brand is to make everyday coffee feel a little more special through thoughtful design and a calm environment.

The visual identity uses deep green, soft cream, and warm gold to create a clean but inviting look. These colours help the brand feel natural, premium, and peaceful at the same time.

Brand Values

  • Quality ingredients and fresh preparation
  • Simple and elegant visual identity
  • Welcoming atmosphere for all visitors
  • Consistency across digital and physical experience

Atmosphere

Soft lighting, clean spaces, and comfortable seating create a place where customers can relax, study, or meet with friends.

Menu Style

The menu focuses on coffee, tea, pastries, and seasonal specials with presentation that matches the café's refined style.

Customer Experience

The goal is to keep the experience simple and welcoming, from the website to the café space.
