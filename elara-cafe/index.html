About Elara Café

Elara Café is a fictional café brand with a soft, clean, and welcoming style. The idea behind the café is to create a quiet place where people can enjoy coffee, study, meet friends, or take a small break.

The design uses simple colours, warm text, and a clean layout to match the calm feeling of the brand.
